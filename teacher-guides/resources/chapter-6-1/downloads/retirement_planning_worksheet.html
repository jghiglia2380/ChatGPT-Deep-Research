Part 3: Reflection and Calculation

The 50/30/20 Rule for Budgeting

The 50/30/20 rule suggests allocating your after-tax income as follows:

  • 50% for needs (housing, food, utilities, etc.)
  • 30% for wants (entertainment, travel, etc.)
  • 20% for savings and debt repayment (including retirement)
