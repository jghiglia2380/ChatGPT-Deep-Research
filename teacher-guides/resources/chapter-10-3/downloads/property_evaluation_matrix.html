Property Evaluation Matrix

Chapter 10.3: Understanding Home Buying

Instructions

This evaluation matrix will help you systematically compare different properties based on financial, location, property, and lifestyle factors. For each property you're considering, assign a rating from 1-5 for each factor, where 1 is poor/unacceptable and 5 is excellent/ideal. You can also adjust the weight of each factor to match your personal priorities.

To save this worksheet as a PDF: Click File → Print → Change destination to "Save as PDF" → Save

Rating Scale Guide

  • 5 - Excellent: Exceeds expectations, ideal situation
  • 4 - Good: Above average, meets needs well
  • 3 - Acceptable: Meets minimum requirements, average
  • 2 - Fair: Below average, has significant concerns
  • 1 - Poor: Does not meet needs, unacceptable

For Weight, assign a number from 1-5 based on how important each factor is to you personally:

  • 5 - Critical: A must-have, deal-breaker factor
  • 3 - Important: Significantly influences decision
  • 1 - Minor: Nice to have but not essential

Weighted Score Calculation
