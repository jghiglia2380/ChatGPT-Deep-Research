Assessment Components and Weighting

Content Quality (40%)

Accuracy of career information, appropriate goals for each time frame, and thoroughness of planning across all categories

Strategic Thinking (30%)

Logical progression from short-term to long-term goals, alignment between education/skills and career aspirations, and coherence across all plan elements

Financial Awareness (20%)

Consideration of education costs, salary progression expectations, and financial implications of career choices

Personalization (10%)

Evidence that career choices reflect student's own interests, strengths, and values rather than generic selections

Scoring Guide

Excellent (90-100%)

  • Thorough and well-researched content with realistic goals for each time frame
  • Strategic plan shows clear progression and alignment between education and career goals
  • Detailed consideration of financial implications with realistic expectations
  • Plan clearly reflects personal interests, strengths, and values

Proficient (80-89%)

  • Appropriate content with realistic goals for most time frames
  • Plan shows reasonable progression with general alignment to career goals
  • Basic consideration of financial implications
  • Plan generally reflects personal preferences

Developing (70-79%)

  • Basic content with some realistic goals, but lacks detail or thoroughness
  • Plan shows some progression but limited strategic alignment
  • Limited consideration of financial implications
  • Some evidence of personalization but includes generic selections

Beginning (Below 70%)

  • Minimal content with vague or unrealistic goals
  • Plan lacks clear progression or alignment between components
  • Minimal or no consideration of financial implications
  • Little evidence of personalization
