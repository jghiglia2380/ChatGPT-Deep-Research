Important Considerations

  • Future Premium Changes: Claims may affect future premiums. Consider long-term impacts when comparing plans.
  • Coverage Limits: Ensure liability limits are adequate for your assets and financial situation.
  • Deductible Savings: If choosing a high-deductible plan, consider setting aside the difference in premiums to cover potential deductible expenses.
  • Policy Exclusions: Review all policy exclusions carefully before making a final decision.
  • Discount Opportunities: Inquire about all available discounts (multi-policy, safe driver, etc.) before finalizing.
