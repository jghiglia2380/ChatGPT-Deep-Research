Strategy Examples (For Reference)

Credit Factor Example Strategies
Payment History (35%)
  • Set up automatic payments for all bills
  • Create calendar reminders for due dates
  • Pay bills as soon as they arrive rather than waiting until due date
  • Contact creditors immediately if you'll be late with a payment
Credit Utilization (30%)
  • Keep credit card balances below 30% of your limit
  • Make multiple payments throughout the month
  • Request credit limit increases (without hard inquiries)
  • Pay off balances in full each month
Length of Credit History (15%)
  • Keep oldest accounts open, even if rarely used
  • Become an authorized user on a parent's established account
  • Open a secured credit card to begin building history
  • Maintain accounts for the long-term rather than frequently opening/closing
Credit Mix (10%)
  • Start with a secured credit card
  • Consider a small installment loan when appropriate
  • Add a retail store card when you're ready for more accounts
  • Only add new credit types when you can responsibly manage them
New Credit (10%)
  • Limit credit applications to one every 6 months
  • Research and apply only for cards you're likely to qualify for
  • Avoid multiple applications when rate shopping (use 14-day window)
  • Focus on building history with existing accounts rather than opening new ones
