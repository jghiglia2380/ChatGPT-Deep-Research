Payment Strategy 1: Bi-weekly Payments

Strategy Description

Instead of making one payment per month (12 payments per year), make half the monthly payment every two weeks (26 payments per year). This results in one extra full payment per year and can significantly reduce both the loan term and total interest paid.

Strategy Description

Make regular monthly payments plus one additional lump sum payment each year (perhaps from a tax refund, bonus, or other windfall). This annual extra payment reduces principal and shortens the loan term.

Payment Strategy 4: Refinancing

Strategy Description

Replace your current loan with a new loan that has a lower interest rate. This strategy can lower your monthly payment, total interest, or both, but may extend the loan term or have associated costs.
