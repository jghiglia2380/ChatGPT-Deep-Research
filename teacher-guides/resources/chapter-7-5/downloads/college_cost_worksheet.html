Introduction

This worksheet will help you analyze college expenses and identify strategies to reduce costs. By minimizing expenses, you can reduce the amount you need to borrow in student loans. Complete this worksheet for the type of institution you're considering attending.

TIP: For each expense category, consider all possible ways to reduce costs. Even small savings add up over 4+ years of college and can significantly reduce your total student loan debt.
