# Additional Resources - Chapter 15.1: Career Exploration and Goal Setting ## Overview This document contains curated resources to supplement Chapter 15.1: Career Exploration and Goal Setting. Resources are organized by category and include a variety of tools, guides, articles, videos, and calculators to help students explore careers, assess their interests and skills, set effective goals, and develop comprehensive career plans. ## Section: Career Exploration Tools *These resources help students discover career options based on their interests, skills, values, and educational goals.* ### Resource 1 - **Title:** O*NET Interest Profiler - **Description:** An interactive assessment tool from the U.S. Department of Labor that helps users identify their work-related interests and how they relate to different careers. After completing the assessment, users can explore careers that match their interest profile and learn about required education, expected earnings, and job outlook. - **Tags:** tool free government assessment career-matching - **URL:** https://www.mynextmove.org/explore/ip ### Resource 2 - **Title:** Bureau of Labor Statistics Occupational Outlook Handbook - **Description:** Comprehensive resource providing detailed information on hundreds of occupations. Each profile includes typical duties, work environment, education requirements, median pay, and job outlook. An essential resource for researching career options with reliable, data-driven information. - **Tags:** guide free government occupation-data statistics - **URL:** https://www.bls.gov/ooh/ ### Resource 3 - **Title:** CareerOneStop Videos - **Description:** Video library featuring over 600 career videos organized by industry. Each short video (1-2 minutes) provides a visual introduction to a specific occupation, showing typical work environments, tasks, and required skills. Particularly helpful for visual learners to get a quick overview of different careers. - **Tags:** video free government career-overview visual-learning - **URL:** https://www.careeronestop.org/Videos/CareerVideos/career-videos.aspx ### Resource 4 - **Title:** MyCareerShines - **Description:** Comprehensive career planning system with assessments for interests, skills, and values. Offers personalized career matches, education planning tools, and a resume builder. Features robust exploration tools that connect assessment results to detailed career information. - **Tags:** tool free assessment career-matching education-planning - **URL:** https://www.floridashines.org/find-a-career/plan-your-future ## Section: Self-Assessment Resources *These resources help students better understand their interests, skills, values, and personality traits as they relate to career choices.* ### Resource 1 - **Title:** Career Values Assessment - **Description:** Interactive assessment tool that helps users identify their core work values. Provides personalized results explaining how these values influence career satisfaction and highlights careers that typically align with particular value sets. - **Tags:** tool free assessment values - **URL:** https://www.123test.com/work-values-test/ ### Resource 2 - **Title:** Skills Matcher - **Description:** Tool from CareerOneStop that helps users identify their skills and matches them to careers that use those skills. Users rate their proficiency in various skill areas, and the tool provides career suggestions that align with their skill profile. - **Tags:** tool free government skills assessment - **URL:** https://www.careeronestop.org/Toolkit/Skills/skills-matcher.aspx ### Resource 3 - **Title:** The Interests Assessment - **Description:** Comprehensive interest inventory based on Holland's six interest areas (Realistic, Investigative, Artistic, Social, Enterprising, and Conventional). Provides detailed results explaining interest patterns and connecting them to potential career paths. - **Tags:** tool free assessment interests holland - **URL:** https://www.truity.com/test/holland-code-career-test ### Resource 4 - **Title:** Transferable Skills Survey - **Description:** Assessment tool that helps users identify transferable skills they've developed through education, work, volunteering, and other activities. Particularly useful for students who may not recognize the valuable skills they already possess. - **Tags:** tool free assessment skills transferable - **URL:** https://www.livecareer.com/resources/careers/planning/transferable-skills-survey ## Section: SMART Goal Setting Resources *Resources that help students understand and apply the SMART framework (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) to career planning.* ### Resource 1 - **Title:** SMART Goals Guide: Examples and Template - **Description:** Comprehensive guide to setting SMART goals with career-specific examples, common pitfalls to avoid, and a downloadable worksheet. Features real-world examples of career goals at different stages, from students to mid-career professionals, and shows the process of refining goals to meet SMART criteria. - **Tags:** video free goal-setting examples - **URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WxDQft-5yx4 ## Section: Career Path Planning Tools *These resources help students develop comprehensive career plans with specific action steps across different timeframes.* ### Resource 1 - **Title:** Career Roadmap Builder - **Description:** Interactive tool that helps users create visual career roadmaps with milestones, skills development, education requirements, and experience goals.
