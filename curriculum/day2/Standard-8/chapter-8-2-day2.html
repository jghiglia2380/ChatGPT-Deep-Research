Chapter 8.2: Online Shopping - Learning Lab

Applying online shopping security concepts through interactive activities

Learning Objectives

By the end of this Learning Lab, you will be able to:

  • Identify security features of legitimate online retailers
  • Compare products across multiple online platforms
  • Analyze checkout processes for security features
  • Understand your rights as an online consumer
  • Create a personalized strategy for safe and effective online shopping

Introduction

Welcome to the Online Shopping Learning Lab! Today, you'll apply what you learned yesterday about online shopping security and smart purchasing decisions through hands-on activities. You'll practice identifying secure websites, compare prices across retailers, analyze checkout processes, and develop your own personal online shopping strategy.

Important Note

The activities in this learning lab provide simulated experiences to help you practice online shopping skills in a safe environment. In real online shopping situations, always be vigilant about protecting your personal and financial information, and remember that security threats constantly evolve.

Connection to Day 1

In Day 1, we explored the benefits and risks of online shopping, including the convenience of 24/7 access, greater selection, and price comparison capabilities, as well as security concerns and potential scams. Today, we'll apply those concepts by practicing how to identify secure websites, compare prices effectively, understand checkout security features, know your consumer rights, and create a personalized shopping strategy.

  • We learned about the indicators of secure websites (HTTPS, padlock icon, etc.)
  • We discussed the importance of researching retailers before making purchases
  • We explored strategies for protecting personal and financial information
  • We considered the benefits and drawbacks of online vs. in-store shopping
