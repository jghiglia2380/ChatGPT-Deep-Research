Chapter 15.1: Career Exploration and Goal Setting

Learning Lab: Personal Career Path Planning

Learning Objectives

  • Apply career exploration insights to personal career planning
  • Develop a comprehensive career path plan with milestones
  • Create strategies for overcoming potential career obstacles
  • Design an accountability system for career goal progress
  • Integrate financial considerations into career planning

Introduction

Welcome to the Learning Lab for Chapter 15.1! Today, we'll apply what we learned about career exploration to develop your personalized career path plan. Through hands-on activities, you'll create a comprehensive career timeline, identify potential obstacles, design an accountability system, and integrate financial considerations into your planning.

Important Note

Your career plan is a living document that will evolve over time. Be thoughtful but not rigid in your planning, as your interests, skills, and opportunities will change throughout your career journey. The goal today is to create a structured but flexible framework that can guide your decisions while adapting to new information.

Connection to Day 1

In Day 1, you explored the difference between jobs and careers, identified your interests, skills, and values, and learned about career research resources. Today, we'll use those insights to create a concrete career path plan. The self-assessments and research you conducted will form the foundation of your planning activities today.
