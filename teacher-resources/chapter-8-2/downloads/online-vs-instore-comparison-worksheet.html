Online vs. In-Store Purchase Analysis

Chapter 8.2: Online Shopping

Instructions

This worksheet will help you analyze whether to purchase a specific product online or in a physical store. Consider all the factors listed below for both online and in-store options, then determine which shopping method would be better for your specific situation.

  1. Select a product you're interested in purchasing
  2. For each factor, note the advantages and disadvantages of both shopping methods
  3. Indicate your preference for each factor in the "My Preference" column
  4. Based on your analysis, determine which option would be better for you overall
  5. Complete the reflection questions to deepen your understanding
