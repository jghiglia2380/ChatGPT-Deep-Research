Chapter 2.3: Understanding Tax Filing Requirements

Introduction

This worksheet helps you determine whether you need to file a federal income tax return based on your personal situation. By working through each section, you'll identify your filing status, income sources, and whether you meet the filing thresholds.

Important: This worksheet is based on general tax filing requirements and should be used for educational purposes only. Tax laws and filing thresholds change annually. Always verify current requirements on the official IRS website at www.irs.gov.
