Key Laws Reference

Use this quick reference to help with your analysis:

  • Truth in Lending Act (TILA): Requires disclosure of loan terms and costs
  • Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA): Governs credit reports and provides rights to dispute errors
  • Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA): Prohibits discrimination in lending
  • Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA): Regulates third-party debt collectors
  • Credit CARD Act: Provides credit card protections, including against retroactive rate increases
  • Dodd-Frank Act / CFPB: Created the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau to enforce laws and protect consumers
