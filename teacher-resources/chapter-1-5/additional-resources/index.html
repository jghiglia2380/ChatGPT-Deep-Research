Additional Resources

Chapter 1.5: Managing Your Income Effectively

This curated collection of resources provides additional tools, guides, and information to help you effectively manage your income. These resources support and extend the concepts covered in Chapter 1.5.

Budgeting Tools & Apps

Tool

Mint

A free budget tracker and planner that helps you manage your money in one place. Features include budget creation, expense tracking, bill payment reminders, and credit score monitoring.
Tool

You Need A Budget (YNAB)

A budgeting app based on the zero-based budgeting method that helps users give every dollar a job. Offers educational resources along with the budgeting tool.
Tool

NerdWallet Budget Worksheet

A free, downloadable budget worksheet that helps you track income, fixed expenses, variable expenses, and savings goals in one comprehensive spreadsheet.

Government & Educational Resources

Guide

Age-appropriate financial education resources to help develop positive financial habits and skills. Includes activities, conversation starters, and teaching guides.

Educational resources on the fundamentals of saving and investing, including compound interest calculators, guidance on emergency funds, and investment basics.

50/30/20 Budget Resources

Tool

NerdWallet 50/30/20 Budget Calculator

Interactive calculator that helps you apply the 50/30/20 rule to your specific income, with customized recommendations for needs, wants, and savings allocations.
Article

Investopedia - What Is the 50/30/20 Budget Rule?

Comprehensive explanation of the 50/30/20 budgeting strategy with examples, benefits, limitations, and how to adapt it to different income levels and life situations.
Video

Two Cents: The 50/30/20 Rule of Money

Engaging 8-minute video explaining the 50/30/20 budget rule with clear examples and visual explanations of how to apply it to everyday financial decisions.
