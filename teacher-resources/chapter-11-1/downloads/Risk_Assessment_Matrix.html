Chapter 11.1: Understanding and Managing Risk

Instructions for Using This Worksheet

This Risk Assessment Matrix will help you identify, categorize, and evaluate risks in your life. Follow these steps:

  1. Identify risks you face across different categories
  2. Rate each risk's likelihood and potential impact on a scale of 1-5
  3. Calculate the risk rating by multiplying likelihood × impact
  4. Place each risk in the appropriate cell of the risk matrix
  5. Determine the most appropriate management strategy for each risk

To save as PDF: Use File → Print, then select "Save as PDF" as the destination.
