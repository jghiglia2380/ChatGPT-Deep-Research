Career-Financial Integration Worksheet

Chapter 15.1: Career Exploration and Goal Setting

Instructions for Using This Worksheet

This worksheet helps you integrate financial planning with your career development. The choices you make in your career path have significant financial implications. Follow these steps:

  1. Research the education and training costs required for your chosen career path
  2. Investigate salary projections at different career stages
  3. Consider benefits beyond salary that impact your financial well-being
  4. Calculate the return on investment for your education/training
  5. Align your financial goals with your career timeline

Helpful Resources for Career-Financial Research

  • Bureau of Labor Statistics (www.bls.gov) - Official salary data and employment projections
  • O*NET OnLine (www.onetonline.org) - Detailed career information including skills and education requirements
  • College Scorecard (collegescorecard.ed.gov) - Education costs and outcomes by institution and program
  • Glassdoor (www.glassdoor.com) - Company reviews, salary information, and interview insights
  • PayScale (www.payscale.com) - Salary data by job title, location, and experience level
